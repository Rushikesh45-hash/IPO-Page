Frequently Asked Questions?

Find answers to common questions that come in your mind related to IPO.

How to Subscribe to an IPO?

  • Step 1: Login to your respective service provider.
  • Step 2: Click on the IPO button.
  • Step 3: Select the IPO you want to bid and enter the relevant details.
  • Step 4: Your subscription will be completed once you make the payment or give permission.
Should I buy an IPO first day?

Buying an IPO on the first day can be risky as prices may fluctuate heavily. It's best to analyze the company's fundamentals and market conditions before investing.

How do you know if an IPO is good?

Check financials, company history, and market demand before investing in an IPO.

How to check IPO start date?

You can check the IPO start date on the stock exchange website or the company's prospectus.

What is issue size?

Issue size refers to the total value of shares issued in an IPO.

How many shares in a lot?

The number of shares in a lot varies from one IPO to another and is decided by the company.

How is the lot size calculated?

Lot size is determined based on the minimum number of shares a retail investor can buy.

Who decides the IPO price band?

The IPO price band is decided by the company and its underwriters based on market demand.

What is IPO GMP?

IPO GMP (Grey Market Premium) is the unofficial premium price at which IPO shares are traded before listing.

How many lots should I apply for IPO?

Apply based on your investment capacity and risk appetite, as allocation is often lottery-based.
